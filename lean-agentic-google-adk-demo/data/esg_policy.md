## 2. Carbon Intensity Screening

Portfolio-weighted average carbon intensity (WACI, tCO2e per $M revenue, Scopes 1+2) must remain below 120. Individual holdings with WACI above 400 are classified HIGH-CARBON and capped at 4.0% portfolio weight each, 10.0% in aggregate. Holdings flagged 'fossil' require a documented transition plan or a scheduled divestment glide path of no more than eight quarters.

## 6. Rebalancing Rules

Rebalances are quarterly by default; event-driven rebalances are permitted where client preferences allow. A rebalance recommendation must include: proposed trades, post-trade weights, WACI before/after, flag exposure before/after, expected tracking error, and a plain-language client summary of no more than 200 words. Cash allocation must remain within 2%-14%.
